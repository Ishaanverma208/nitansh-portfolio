Process

Research Methodology

A structured, four-phase approach to every valuation engagement.

01

Industry & Company Deep Dive

Comprehensive analysis of the automotive value chain, competitive positioning, regulatory environment, and company-specific KPIs — sales volumes, ASP, production capacity, electrification roadmap.

02

Financial Statement Modelling

Building a fully integrated 3-statement model (Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow) with granular revenue build-up by segment and geography, capturing cyclical patterns specific to automotive demand.

03

Valuation Framework Application

Applying the appropriate valuation method(s) — DCF, trading comps, precedent transactions, or LBO — and deriving a valuation range with a Football Field chart to frame the investment decision.

04

Scenario Analysis & Reporting

Stress-testing the model with bull / base / bear scenarios, sensitivity tables, and key risk factors. Presenting findings in a concise, institutional-quality equity research report.
